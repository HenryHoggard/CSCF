    The 2nd Annual CSCf Hackathon - Our first event of 2013!

    The next CSCf Hackathon, hosted at Cardiff University School of Computer Science and Informatics will be on the weekend 20-21st April 2013.

    We'll be providing a friendly atmosphere with free food and drink over the course of two days, so that you can hack something awesome together in that time!

    Prizes will also be provided for the best projects (best overall project, and best overall project with use of automation).

    All we ask in return is that you open-source your code after the event.
