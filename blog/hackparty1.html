HackParty

0x01 ~ Hardware Hax

21-22 October 2012

We are trialing HackParty as a new and hopefully regular hackathon. This will be a smaller, more informal hackathon, hosted in a house. The theme for the first event is Hardware Hax, so prepare your Pi's and assemble your Arduinos!

Here are some guidelines on what to bring...
  • Laptops & Power Cables
  • Your favourite caffenated energy drinks
  • Your favourite alcohol beverages
  • Money for pizza
  • Food
  • Sleeping bag
