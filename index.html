    About

    Computer Science Cardiff (CSCf) is a portal to all things happening in the Computer Science community at Cardiff. We also put on extra curricular events for students: In March 2012 we brought you The Open Sauce Hackathon, and in the 2012/2013 year we aim to bring a new range of events from Hackathons to workshops.

    Why Github?

    We students are only at university for 3 or 4 years, therefore by using github, even after we leave university, the new students can maintain this website and keep it updated. Github also provides a free and reliable platform for hosting this website
