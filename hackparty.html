    The CSCf HackParty - 19-21st October 2012

    The HackParty was an informal two-day hardware hackathon, where anything and everything could be hacked - from wireless headsets to Raspberry Pis and Arduinos. Eventually, a Wi-Fi enabled kettle was born. A weekend of late nights, pizza, energy drinks, and beer.
